I grew up just outside Boston, Massachusetts.

If you've never been to Boston (which you should, it's a beautiful city), then you wouldn't know how ingrained the sports culture is there. It's completely illogical, but if I meet a Yankees fan, my first impression of them won't be good. Every kid has a favorite Sox player, every Monday is dedicated to talking about the Pats game. When the Bruins or Celtics are in the playoffs, the city doesn't sleep.

Growing up in that environment, was the best. But, I developed an unexplainable connection to those teams. When the Sox aren't playing well, or the Pats lose at the last second, it physically hurts. But when the Pats win (another) Super Bowl, or the Sox are top in the AL East, I'm through the roof.

That's the magic.
